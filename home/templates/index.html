Heit Horseradish: “Health in all savour”

Our family has been growing horseradish for several years in Létavértes, the homeland of Hungarian horseradish and since 2012, we have been processing the excellent quality roots on a small scale. Hungarian horseradish is a hungaricum, which has been declared and protected by the European Commission since 2004.

Our products do not contain preservatives or other artificial additives. Our horseradish is also available in a sugar-free version. Choose from our unique horseradish repertoire, which we make according to our family recipes, at the highest practical level of handicraft products!

About the horseradish

Initially, horseradish was not even considered an aromatic herb, it was mainly used only in folk medicine. It is high in sodium, calcium, magnesium and vitamin C and is also known, for its preventive effects on health. It is excellent for stomach aches, helps digestion and brings appetite. More and more people believe that horseradish also has antiviral and bactericidal effects.

It is a bad habit, that grated horseradish is usually consumed only during Easter in just few European countries. Horseradish can serve our body particularly well during vitamin-deficient months of winter and spring, and it harmonizes perfectly with almost any meat dish.

Instead of medications, use a natural immune booster especially if it is within reach and also delicious!
